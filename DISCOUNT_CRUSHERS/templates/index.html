Decision rule

We predict upgrade probability with discount=0 and discount=1 using your fitted logistic regression. If discount lifts probability to ≥ 50% (and baseline < 50%), we say Offer discount. If baseline is already ≥ 50%, we say No discount needed. Otherwise, Not sufficient.
